Broadway Cafe has been in business since 1952, the cafe offers many different kinds of coffees, teas, a full service bakery, homemade soups, sandwiches, and salads.

Events like live music and book clubs are held regularly at the Café on weekends.

Do not forget to make a reservation in advance for any activity you want to do in the café.
